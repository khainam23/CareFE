# Product Overview

CareFE is a caregiver marketplace platform connecting customers seeking care services with professional caregivers. The application supports two primary user types:

- **Customers**: Individuals seeking care services who can browse, schedule, and manage caregivers
- **Caregivers/Employees**: Professional care providers who can manage their profiles, schedules, tasks, and payment history

## Core Features

- User authentication and registration for both customer and caregiver roles
- Caregiver discovery and search functionality
- Profile management for both user types
- Scheduling and care management
- Dashboard views with role-specific information
- Payment history tracking
- Task management for caregivers
